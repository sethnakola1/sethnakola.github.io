Your Health Data, Our Priority
Last Updated: October 24, 2025
Important Notice: DrCareAI is committed to protecting your health information in compliance with HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act), GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation), and other applicable privacy laws. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal health information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Health Information

  • Symptoms and Medical History: Information you provide about your health conditions, symptoms, and medical background
  • Diagnostic Data: Results from any assessments or analyses conducted through our AI system
  • Treatment Preferences: Your preferences regarding healthcare options and recommendations

1.2 Personal Information

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number (if provided)
  • Demographics: Age, gender, location (for relevant health recommendations)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, usage analytics

1.3 Usage Data

  • Platform interaction patterns
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Feature usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Healthcare Purposes

  • AI-Powered Analysis: To provide personalized health recommendations and insights
  • Symptom Assessment: To analyze your symptoms and suggest appropriate next steps
  • Health Education: To provide relevant health information and resources

2.2 Platform Improvement

  • Enhancing AI accuracy and reliability
  • Improving user experience and interface
  • Developing new health assessment features

2.3 Communication

  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests
  • Sending important health-related updates (with your consent)
  • Notifying about platform updates or policy changes

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal health information to third parties.

3.1 Limited Sharing Scenarios

  • Healthcare Providers: Only with your explicit consent and for your direct care
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or regulatory authorities
  • Emergency Situations: To prevent serious harm to your health or safety
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist in platform operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

4. Data Security Measures

4.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Access Controls: Multi-factor authentication and role-based access limitations
  • Regular Audits: Continuous security monitoring and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure Hosting: Data hosted on HIPAA-compliant, SOC 2 certified servers

4.2 Administrative Safeguards

  • Employee training on privacy and security protocols
  • Regular security policy updates and compliance reviews
  • Incident response procedures
  • Business Associate Agreements with all service providers

4.3 Physical Safeguards

  • Secure data centers with biometric access controls
  • Environmental monitoring and protection
  • Secure disposal of hardware and media

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal health information for the minimum time necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account remains active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data may be retained for up to 7 years after last activity (as required by healthcare regulations)
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal or regulatory requirements
  • Anonymized Data: De-identified data may be retained indefinitely for research and platform improvement

6. International Data Transfers

If you are accessing DrCareAI from outside India, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in India. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

  • Adherence to applicable data protection laws
  • Implementation of appropriate technical and organizational measures
  • Use of standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions where applicable

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control Rights

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing for direct marketing or legitimate interests

7.2 How to Exercise Rights

To exercise these rights or make inquiries about your personal information, please contact our Privacy Officer using the information provided below.

8. Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach involving personal health information, we will:

  • Notify affected individuals within 60 days of discovery
  • Report to relevant authorities within required timeframes
  • Provide details about the breach and steps being taken
  • Offer appropriate assistance and remediation

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or applicable laws. We will notify users of material changes through our platform and by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
